About Me!

Hi there! It's nice to meet you! My name is Santiago Benitez-Fernandez. I'm currently a 2nd year student at the Rochester Institute of Technology! I've been making games in various aspects for the better part of a decade, whether it be making card games with my friends, playing video games since I was a little kid, or learning about Video Game Development in high school, games have been an aspect of me throughout it all.
