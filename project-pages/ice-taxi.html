Project Description

Made over the course of about 3 weeks by myself for the 2026 Winter Game Jam using premade 3D models and assets from various open source sites.

Oh no! The town's snowed in! But the needs of people waits for no one, and you're the only person with a car good enough to traverse these icy streets.

You must drift from location to location, getting people to their destination. Use whatever route you can find, even if it ends up being across the icy lake! Anything to make it before your tank of gas runs out.
