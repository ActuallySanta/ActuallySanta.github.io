Project Description

Don't Lose Your Head is a puzzle platformer made in a week with a team of 3 other programmers and 1 artist for the 2025 Scream Jam.

Play as a skeleton collecting as they navigate a haunted manor utilizing the fact that they can throw their head to activate conveniently placed levers and buttons to collect candy for their friends!
